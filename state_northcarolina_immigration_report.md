## Voting System Design

### Methodology: Hybrid Approval Voting with Adaptive Weighting

**Voting Method**: Hybrid Approval Voting  
**Weighting Formula**: `Weight = Base × (1 + Expertise × 0.3) × (1 + Proximity × 0.2) × (1 + Participation × 0.15)`  
**Fairness Constraints**: Min 30% satisfaction, Max 40% disparity

#### Weight Components

1. **Base Weight**: 1.0 for all registered voters
2. **Expertise Multiplier**: Based on professional background (education, certifications)
   - Immigration law: 1.3
   - Economics: 1.2
   - Public health: 1.2
   - Law enforcement: 1.25
   - General citizen: 1.0

3. **Proximity Multiplier**: Based on geographic impact
   - Border counties: 1.3
   - Urban centers: 1.1
   - Rural areas: 1.0
   - Statewide impact: 1.0

4. **Participation Multiplier**: Based on voting history
   - 4+ recent elections: 1.15
   - 2-3 recent elections: 1.10
   - 1 recent election: 1.05
   - No recent elections: 1.0

#### Voting Process

**Tier 1 - County Level** (Voting Period: 14 days)
- Citizens vote on policies affecting their county
- Weighted approval voting: Yes/No with weights
- Policies with >50% approval and >30% satisfaction move to Tier 2

**Tier 2 - State Level** (Voting Period: 21 days)
- Representatives from each county vote on Tier 1 approved policies
- Weighted by county population and satisfaction scores
- Policies with >60% approval and >40% satisfaction move to Tier 3

**Tier 3 - Citizen Referendum** (Voting Period: 28 days)
- All citizens vote on Tier 2 approved policies
- Policies with >55% approval and >35% satisfaction implemented
- Minimum 30% turnout required for validity
