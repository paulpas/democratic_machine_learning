# Multi-Perspective Critique Execution Plan

## Overview
This plan executes real internet research, cross-referencing, and analysis for democratic decision-making. Each step requires actual data collection and verification.

## Phase 1: Data Collection (30-60 minutes)

### Internet Sources to Scrape:
1. **Polling Data**
   - Gallup polling archive
   - Pew Research Center
   - CNN/FOX/ABC/NBC polls
   - State-level polling data

2. **Social Media Analysis**
   - Twitter/X political discourse
   - Reddit political subreddits
   - Facebook political groups
   - YouTube political commentary

3. **News Media Analysis**
   - Major news outlets (NYT, WaPo, WSJ, CNN, FOX, etc.)
   - Local news coverage
   - Media bias ratings (Media Bias/Fact Check)

4. **Government Data**
   - Census Bureau demographic data
   - IRS tax data on income distribution
   - Bureau of Labor Statistics
   - Department of Homeland Security immigration data

5. **Academic Research**
   - Political science journals
   - Economics research papers
   - Sociology studies
   - Historical governance analyses

## Phase 2: Multi-Perspective Analysis (4-6 hours)

### For Each Topic (Immigration, Election Policy, etc.):

**12 Perspectives to Analyze:**
1. Low-income/disadvantaged populations
2. High-income/privileged populations
3. Political scientists
4. Economists
5. Sociologists
6. Historians
7. Legal scholars
8. Business owners/employers
9. Workers/unions
10. Religious leaders
11. Environmental advocates
12. Technology experts

### For Each Perspective:
- **Primary stance**: What do they want?
- **Key concerns**: What keeps them up at night?
- **Policy preferences**: Specific policy positions
- **Potential impacts**: How will policies affect them?
- **Historical parallels**: Similar situations in history
- **Data sources**: Polling, studies, expert opinions

## Phase 3: Cross-Reference Analysis (8-12 hours)

### 12×12 Matrix = 144 Comparisons Per Topic

**For Each Comparison:**
1. Identify agreements
2. Identify contradictions
3. Calculate consensus score (0-100%)
4. Find common ground
5. Document reasoning for each finding
6. Cross-reference with real data

### Example Comparisons:
- Low-income vs High-income on immigration
- Political scientists vs Business owners on visa systems
- Religious leaders vs Technology experts on privacy

## Phase 4: Anti-Search & Verification (6-8 hours)

### For Each Perspective:
1. Find strongest counter-arguments
2. Search for evidence against their stance
3. Verify claims with multiple sources
4. Detect misinformation/bias
5. triangulate with independent data

### Verification Methods:
- Cross-reference with 3+ sources
- Check historical precedents
- Analyze economic models
- Review sociological studies
- Examine historical outcomes

## Phase 5: Social Science Integration (4-6 hours)

### For Each Policy Area:
1. **Political Science**: Democracy models, voting theory, institutional design
2. **Economics**: Market effects, incentives, fiscal impact
3. **Sociology**: Group dynamics, inequality, social capital
4. **Psychology**: Cognitive biases, risk perception, decision-making
5. **History**: Past successes/failures, comparative analysis

## Phase 6: Holistic Filter (2-4 hours)

### Check for:
1. Cross-topic correlations
2. Systemic dependencies
3. Policy coherence
4. Long-term sustainability
5. Unintended consequences

## Phase 7: Policy Synthesis (2-4 hours)

### Generate:
1. Functional policy recommendations
2. Weighted voting systems
3. Implementation frameworks
4. Accountability mechanisms
5. Feedback loops

## Total Estimated Time: 25-40 hours

## Resource Requirements:
- Internet access for data collection
- LLM access for analysis (Qwen3-Coder-Next)
- Data storage for results
- Processing power for parallel tasks

## Output:
- Detailed analysis reports
- Cross-reference matrices
- Consensus scores
- Policy recommendations
- LLM call logs with reasoning steps

## Quality Assurance:
- Verify all claims with multiple sources
- Document all reasoning steps
- Include confidence scores
- Flag areas needing further research
- Ensure 95%+ coverage of relevant data
